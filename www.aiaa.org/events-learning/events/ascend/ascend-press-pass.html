ASCEND Press Pass

Thank you for your interest in covering AIAA's upcoming event! To apply for press credentials, please complete and submit the form below. All required fields must be completed in order for your press registration to be processed.

Please note: Marketing/advertising, sales, art directors, managers, publishers, and other non-editorial professionals of media organizations are NOT eligible for press credentials and should proceed to www.ascend.events/registration.

After we have received your press credential request, it will be reviewed and are subject to approval. You will be notified via email within one week of the status of your request. Submitting the press credential registration form does not guarantee that you will receive a press credential. Please DO NOT make your travel arrangements before you are notified that your credential has been approved.
